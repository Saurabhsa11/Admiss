FAQ
Common Questions
Is AdmissionFinder only for Germany?
+
Currently, our database covers German public universities comprehensively. We index programs across TUM, LMU, KIT, TU Berlin, RWTH Aachen, and 40+ other institutions. Expansion to Austria and the Netherlands is planned.
How accurate is the German GPA conversion?
+
We use the official Bavarian formula (also called the Modified Bavarian Formula) used by most German universities: 1 + 3 × (Max GPA − Your GPA) ÷ (Max GPA − Min GPA). This is the same formula DAAD and most admissions portals use. You input your GPA, your scale's min and max, and we compute it precisely.
Why is my profile locked after the first search?
+
Your GPA, credits, and academic background are locked to your account to prevent profile manipulation between searches. Only your target course stays editable — so you can explore multiple fields without re-entering your academic data each time.
What does the gap analysis actually show?
+
For each matched program, we compare your completed subjects (derived from your degree background and ECTS credits) against the program's stated prerequisites. We flag missing subject areas, credit shortfalls, and language requirement gaps — and indicate which are hard requirements vs. which programs accept applications with explanations.
Is my data secure? Who can see it?
+
Your locked profile is stored in your browser's localStorage, not on our servers. Search queries are sent to our API for matching, but we do not store or share your personal academic data with universities, agents, or advertisers. Guest sessions use sessionStorage, which clears when you close the tab.
What if I'm not satisfied with the results?
+
We offer a full refund within 7 days of purchase if you feel the report didn't provide value. Email us with your registered email and we'll process it — no questions asked.

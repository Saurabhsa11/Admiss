Pricing Questions
Commonly Asked
What's the difference between AI Matching and Custom College Pack?
+
AI Matching is for students who don't have a college list yet — our algorithm finds your best-fit programs from 5,000+ options. Custom College Pack is for students who already know which universities they want to target. You give us the colleges, we build a detailed eligibility and requirement report specifically for those colleges.
Why is the AI Matching plan ₹249 now but ₹999 later?
+
We're in our founding phase — the first 500 users get lifetime access to AI matching at ₹249 as a reward for being early adopters. Once those 500 slots fill, the plan reverts to its regular price of ₹999. The founding price is a permanent benefit for early members, not a temporary discount that resets.
Can I use both AI Matching AND a Custom College Pack?
+
Yes — and this is the most effective approach. Use AI Matching first to discover programs you might not have considered, then use the Custom Pack to get a deep-dive report on specific colleges from that list (plus any you already had in mind). The plans complement each other.
What exactly does the consultancy ₹15,000 Phase 1 fee include?
+
Phase 1 covers everything needed to prepare and submit a strong application — a shortlist of 20+ matched programs from 1,300+ courses, full document preparation (SOP strategy, LOR guidance, CV, transcripts), per-college requirement reports, and German GPA conversion. It is non-refundable because it represents real consultant time and expert analysis delivered upfront.
If I buy the AI report at ₹249, can I upgrade to full consultancy?
+
Yes. If you purchase the AI Matching plan (₹299 founding / ₹999 after) and later decide you want full consultancy, we credit that amount against Phase 1. So you'd pay ₹15,000 minus ₹299 (or ₹999), not the full amount again. We designed it this way because understanding your profile first leads to better consultancy outcomes for everyone.
Is there a refund on the Custom College Pack plans?
+
Yes — 7-day full refund if you feel the report didn't provide value. Email your registered address and we'll process it, no questions asked. We can't offer refunds after 7 days as reports are custom-built for your profile and college list.
